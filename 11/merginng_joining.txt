When you work with large dataset often times you need to merge them
Merging means do ya do se jyada data frames ki rows ko combine karna based on a common key column
pd.merge(df1, df2, on="Commo_column_name", how="Type of join")
Type of joins - 
    inner : join only common rows
    outer : join everything and put NaN where there is no data
    left : left ke saare data hone chahiye and jiska right exist nhi krta uska NaN
    right : vice-versa
    cross : Agr ek df mein m rows hai and nother mein n rows then yeh m x n return krega
